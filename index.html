Computer Devices


Input Devices 
1. Keyboard - Allows users to input text, numbers, and commands into a computer by pressing keys.
2. Mouse - Enables users to interact with a computer by moving a pointer on the screen and selecting items.
3. Scanner - Digitizes physical documents and images into electronic format for computer processing.
4. Microphone - Captures audio input, such as voice or music, and converts it into a digital signal for the computer.
Output Devices 
1. Monitor - Displays visual information from the computer, allowing users to interact with graphical interfaces.
2. Printer - Produces hard copies of digital documents and images on paper.
3. Speakers - Convert digital audio signals into sound that can be heard by the user.
4. Projector - Projects images and videos onto a large surface, typically a screen or wall, for presentations or viewing.
